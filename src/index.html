Design

At Profital, our development team works directly with you to design the perfect website for your needs.

1
Wireframing

Using an iterative process, we work with you to build a map of your website, and the layout for each page.
All of the features you need are accounted for!
Intuitive design principles are always respected to make it easy and FUN for users to explore your site!

2
Styling

Next we find an overall theme to represent your business.
Consistent colors, fonts, animations, images, and other style components will inspire your audience to explore, and make your services stand out!

3
Content Curation

Send us all the media that you want on the site - images, audio, video, promotions, and whatever else you want to add. No idea is too grand!
